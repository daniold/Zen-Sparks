How to Play

Goal: Your goal is to generate Energy by having your Sparks hit the central Heart Crystal.

Set a unique name to appear on the leaderboard!

Sparks: Buy more Sparks to hit the crystal more often. More Sparks = More Energy!

Power: Upgrade Power to increase the amount of Energy you get from each hit.

Speed: Upgrade Speed to make your Sparks move faster, resulting in more hits per second.

Crit %: Upgrade your Critical Hit chance. Crits give a massive Energy bonus!

Ascension: Once you've earned enough Energy, you can Ascend. This resets your progress but grants you a permanent boost to all future Energy gains, allowing you to progress even faster!
